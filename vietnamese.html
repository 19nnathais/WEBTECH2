Vietnamese

Vietnamese music is a diverse blend of traditional, modern, and Western musical styles that reflects the country's rich cultural heritage. Here are the best stars who are redefining the music genres in Vietnam.

10 Artists

Sơn Tùng M-TP Pop R&B, Rap, EDM

Son Tung M-TP is a singer, songwriter, and actor. He is known for his unique style that combines pop and EDM genres. He rose to fame with his hit songs "Em Cua Ngay Hom Qua" and "Noi Nay Co Anh" and has since become one of the most successful and influential artists in Vietnam's music industry.

Mono R&B, Hiphop, Pop

Mono is the rising star of Vietnamese music. Son Tung M-TP's younger brother has truly stepped out of the shadow of his older brother, becoming one of the most promising male artists in the Vietnamese music scene. The standout tracks in his first modern R&B and hip-hop album are "Em La" and "Waiting For You."

Bích Phương Pop

Bich Phuong is a popular Vietnamese singer known for her unique vocal style and catchy pop songs. She rose to fame in 2016 with the hit song "Bao Gio Lay Chong" and has since released multiple successful albums and singles, earning her a loyal fanbase in Vietnam and beyond.

Tlinh Ballad, Dance/Electronic, Hip Hop

tlinh is a dynamic and talented young rapper, within a short time of joining rap, she has made fantastic progress. She has a clever way of rhyming, playing with words, rapping, and singing, which is very smooth and attractive. tlinh has viral songs such as: "Thích quá rùi nà", "Gái Độc Thân", "ghệ iu dấu của em ơi", etc.

Low G Rap

Low G is a famous rapper in Vietnam. He has a distinctive rap voice as well as a smooth rap ability that is superior to many new-generation young rappers. Some of his typical tracks are: "Đơn Giản", "Càng Cua", "Okeokeoke", etc.

Soobin Hoàng Sơn Pop, Ballad, RnB, Rap, EDM

Soobin Hoang Son is a male singer, songwriter, rapper, and music producer under the SpaceSpeakers Group company in Vietnam. He is one of the few male singers in Vietnam who has the ability to show many genres of music and outstanding stage performance skills. He is famous for "Phía Sau Một Cô Gái", "Ngày Mai Em Đi"and "Tháng Năm".

Vũ Indie Pop, Acoustic, Rock

Hoang Thai Vu commonly known by his stage name Vu (stylized as Vũ.), is a Vietnamese singer-songwriter. Despite not appearing widely on mainstream media, Vu is still one of the most beloved names in the Vietnamese indie music community. Some of his notable hits are "La Lung" and "Buoc Qua Nhau"

HaiSam Hip Hop, Dance/Electronic, R&B, Indie Pop

HaiSam is a male singer known to the audience through the program "Sing my song - The best song of 2018". At the age of 20, the songs that Hai Sam sings have completely different colors and sounds. The audience remembers Hai Sam through songs such as: "Thế thôi", "Cuối Chiều", "Một Điều Mà Anh Rất Ngại Nói Ra".

Mỹ Tâm Pop, Dance, R&B

My Tam is one of the most successful Vietnamese singers for two decades (2000s and 2010s). Her voice is deep, powerful and clear, that fits her into a variety of genres, including pop, R&B and ballad. Her ninth album, "Tâm 9" (2017), even reached number nine on Billboard's World Album Chart, making her the highest ranking Vietnamese singer of all time.

Trịnh Công Sơn Pop, Bolero

Trinh Cong Son (1939 - 2001) was a Vietnamese musician, songwriter, painter and poet. He is widely considered to be Vietnam's best songwriter. His music explores themes of love, loss, and anti-war sentiments during the Vietnam War. The music of Trinh Cong Son is so unique that it has a genre named after him, called "Trinh music," with over 600 songs.

The collaboration with Snoop Dogg brought Sơn Tùng M-TP's music to a global audience, and the song was well-received in many countries outside of Vietnam, including the United States. This also helped to establish Sơn Tùng M-TP as one of the leading figures in the Vietnamese music industry.

Bùa Yêu" is a popular Vietnamese song performed by Bích Phương, a talented singer and songwriter. The song was released in 2017 and quickly became a hit in Vietnam. The song has a catchy melody and upbeat tempo that combines traditional Vietnamese music with modern pop elements. It talks about the feeling of being in love and how it can be like a magical spell, or "bùa yêu" in Vietnamese, that makes people feel happy and complete. The lyrics are poetic and romantic, expressing the joy and excitement of falling in love. "Bùa Yêu" has gained widespread popularity not only in Vietnam but also among Vietnamese communities around the world. The music video for the song has garnered millions of views on YouTube, making it one of the most successful songs by Bích Phương to date.

"Người hãy quên em đi" is a famous ballad of singer My Tam, released in 2011. The song was composed by musician Duc Tri and written by songwriter Ha Cong Tuan. The song revolves around a sad love story, where a person had loved and hoped for that love to continue, but eventually had to accept the truth that the love no longer exists. The lyrics of the song are like advice from an ex-lover to a new one, hoping they can forget the past and start a new life. "Người hãy quên em đi" quickly became one of My Tam's most famous ballads, bringing her back to the position of "queen of Vietnamese ballads". In addition, this song is also considered one of the best works of Duc Tri and Ha Cong Tuan.
